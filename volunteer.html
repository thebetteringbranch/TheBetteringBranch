WHY YOU SHOULD VOLUNTEER WITH US

  1. We are 100% led by youths! (We know what Singapore school culture is like today & how it feels to juggle school alongside everything else)
  2. We offer a wide variety of activities that cater to a even wider variety of schedules. From one-off ad-hoc activities & projects that happen on weekends or holidays to weekly volunteering programs, we have something for everyone.
  3. Expand your network! Meet like-minded individuals from different schools & of different ages, all with the same goal of doing good :)

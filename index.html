WHO ARE WE

The Bettering Branch (Est. 2024) is a youth-founded and led volunteering organisation, that hopes to help better Singapore one branch at a time by empowering youths to give back to society.

OUR STORY SO FAR...

As youths in Singapore with hectic schedules, we often felt that we never had enough time or resources to help the communities we cared about. We also noticed that many of our peers lacked opportunities to learn about and truly understand the depth of certain issues in Singapore, despite their willingness to do so.

This left us feeling helpless and unsure of how we could make a meaningful impact on society as mere students. Hence, The Bettering Branch was born.

OUR MISSION

  1. Educate youths on the importance of today’s issues
  2. Lead & champion change among youths
  3. Provide flexible volunteering opportunities
    (So YOU can do good without the fear of commitment)

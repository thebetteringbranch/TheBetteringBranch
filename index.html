WHO ARE WE

The Bettering Branch is a youth-led non-profit organisation, that hopes to help better Singapore one branch at a time by empowering empathy in youths, encouraging them to give back to society

WHERE OUR WORK MEETS THE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS (SDGs)

SDG 3: GOOD HEALTH & WELL-BEING
We promote community wellness through initiatives like The Bettering Run, a 3.5km community run and carnival that saw close to 300 participants show up for Health! The event also featured educational booths on healthy living done by our team, as well as booths by youth projects, organisations and businesses that uphold healthy living

SDG 4: QUALITY EDUCATION
Mission Lighthouse, our weekly academic tutoring program for primary schoolers (aged 7-12) ensures equitable learning opportunities are available for all students

SDG 12: RESPONSIBLE CONSUMPTION & PRODUCTION
We promote a circular economy through participating in outreach events like The Wasteless Utopia where we spread awareness about sustainable fashion policies & the circular economy & start initiatives like Recycle Red! which diverts huge amounts of used red packets from landfills by encouraging students to recycle instead, reducing waste.

SDG 13: CLIMATE ACTION
Beyond recycling, we engage in environmental advocacy through events like the Sustainable Singapore Gallery Flagship Event where we educated the public about Singapore's unique sustainable energy policies compared to other countries such as the UK & US

SDG 17:PARTNERSHIPS FOR THE GOALS
We frequently partner with other corporate organisations and youth projects, demostrating our use of collective action to solve complex community problems.

OUR STORY SO FAR...

As youths in Singapore with hectic schedules, stemming from a competitive academic & social culture, CCA commitments, and exams that seem to never end, trying to find time to give back to society often equalled sacrificing our academics or health. This meant not a lot of youths cared very much about giving back to society.

Feeling helpless and unsure of how to solve the growing youth apathy surrounding us, we decided to build what we could not find: a platform that allowed students (like ourselves) to give back, without trading our grades or health, and to empower other youths with the empathy to do the same.

In January 2024, with no funding or institutional support, driven solely by conviction and a dream, The Bettering Branch was officially born.

Since then, we have only grown exponentially, surpassing our own expectations and goals. As of our last count, we have mobilised more than 600 volunteers We look forward to what 2026 brings and to continue branching out for the better!

What We Do

Making a difference, one branch at a time

RECYCLE RED!

We started Recycle Red!, a Chinese New Year sustainability initiative which seeked to promote sustainable & eco-conscious habits during Chinese New Year by partnering with Geneco SG to bring Red Packet Recycling bins to Schools. Recycle Red! was also an inter-school competition, with the winning school, Anderson Serangoon Junior College, recycling 80 kilograms worth of red packets and winning a visit from the Milo Van.


We recycled a total of 435 kilograms worth of red packets across 13 Junior Colleges!

THE BETTERING RUN

We partnered with Canberra Community Club to organise a 3.5KM Run + Post-Run Carnival supporting Health & Sustainability. Set against the picturesque backdrop of Canberra Park, The Bettering Run saw more than 200 participants of all ages show up and was an unforgettable morning full of fun!

Find the run website, made courtesy of BytePenguin here: https://thebetteringrun.vercel.app & the event booklet here: https://tinyurl.com/tbreventbooklet

THE WASTELESS UTOPIA

We were invited to The Wasteless Utopia, an event held @ SCAPE* in conjunction with the Global Fashion Revolution Week, to share about Fashion Sustainability Policies in Singapore! To tie in with this year's theme of “Think Globally, Act Locally”, we also shared comparisons between Singapore’s Fashion Sustainability Policies VS Other countries like the UK & US, as well as what we can do locally to embrace Sustainable Fashion.

MISSION LIGHTHOUSE

We collaborated with Project InspiringJoy to bring Mission Lighthouse, a weekly tutoring programme that aims at helping children via tutoring sessions & engagement in meaningful activities.

OUTREACH TO SCHOOLS

We visited the Values-In-Action (VIA) fairs of Tampines Meridian Junior College (TMJC) & Victoria Junior College (VJC), and Jurong Pioneer Junior College (JPJC)'s Green Bazaar to recruit more volunteers & spread awareness for Youth Volunteerism!

WITH PROJECT ALBEDO: THRIFT STORE POP-UP TO SUPPORT FASHION SUSTAINABILITY

We collaborated with Project Albedo to help get volunteers for their Thrift Store Pop-Up in support of reducing textile waste

INFOGRAPHIC POSTS ON INSTAGRAM

As part of our mission to "Educate youths on the importance of today's issues", we post educational infographic posts on topics such as Food Insecurity, Pollution, Animal Rights & more. Through our posts, we have gained more than 230K views on our page! (Last updated 15 April 2025)

COMMUNITY CLEAN-UPS @ LOCAL PARKS/BEACHES

Doing our part to keep SG green & clean via our Community Clean-Ups

ACTIVITIES WITH SENIORS @ FENGSHAN CC’s SENIOR LUNCHEONS

We planned and conducted activities for seniors during Fengshan Community Club’s Senior Luncheons! Some activities include DIY pen holders using recycled plastic bottles + yarn and canvas bag drawing!

FOOD DELIVERY TO HOMEBOUND SENIORS IN THE EAST

Working with Bethesda Care Services to deliver dinner to homebound seniors!

FLEA MARKET FUNDRAISER

We sold handmade bracelets and preloved items like books and clothes in an effort to promote sustainability at the Sembawang GRC National Day Observance Ceremony & Fun walk!

ARTS & CRAFTS WITH KIDS

We organised arts and crafts workshops for kids to provide them an afternoon of fun and learning! Some activities include making their very own slime and stickers

COLLABORATION WITH YOUTH FOR CAUSES (YFC) PROJECT JOEY

We collaborated with Youth For Causes (YFC) Project Joey to help fundraise for SHINE Children & Care Services by selling crochet flowers, coasters and ribbon flowers to the public. We raised more than 2.5K together!

COLLABORATION WITH PANGEA KIDS BURBANK CALIFORNIA

Our outreach goes beyond Singapore's borders! We collaborated with Pangea Kids Burbank from California, USA to create an informative instagram post in order to raise awareness for the current health, human rights and human trafficking concerns in Vietnam.
